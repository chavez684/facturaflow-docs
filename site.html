FAQ

Common questions.

Does FacturaFlow work with SAT CFDI 4.0? +
Yes. FacturaFlow is built specifically for CFDI 4.0. We validate all required fields including UsoCFDI, RegimenFiscalReceptor, DomicilioFiscalReceptor, Exportacion, and all new 4.0 mandatory fields. We also handle CFDI 3.3 for legacy invoices.
How does the CargoWise integration work? +
FacturaFlow connects directly to your CargoWise SQL database. When an invoice is approved, it automatically creates or updates the AP transaction in CargoWise using your configured GL field mappings. No manual exports, no scripts to run — it happens in real time.
Is my data stored in Mexico or abroad? +
Your invoice data is stored on servers in Mexico (or nearest region, configurable). We comply with LFPDPPP (Ley Federal de Protección de Datos Personales). Enterprise customers can choose on-premise deployment.
Can I try it before paying? +
Absolutely. Every plan starts with a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You get full access to all features on your chosen plan. You can also interact with our live prototype right now without signing up.
Does it work for customs brokers (agentes aduanales)? +
Yes. FacturaFlow supports the AP workflows common in customs brokerage: pedimento-linked invoices, DTA charges, handling fees, and customs duty reimbursements. We also handle both freight forwarder and customs broker mixed operations.
What's the implementation timeline? +
Most teams are live within 1-2 days for the Starter plan. Professional with CargoWise integration typically takes 3-5 days including GL field mapping. Enterprise with custom integrations is scoped per project — usually 2-4 weeks.
