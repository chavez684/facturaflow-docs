Frequently Asked Questions
What happens when I exceed my invoice limit?
When you reach your monthly invoice limit, new invoice uploads will be paused and your team will receive an email notification. You can either wait until the next billing cycle resets your count, or upgrade your plan immediately to continue processing. We never delete invoices — all data is safely retained.
Can I change plans mid-cycle?
Yes. When upgrading, you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing period, and your new limits take effect immediately. When downgrading, the change will apply at the start of your next billing cycle and you'll receive a credit for unused days on your current plan.
Is there a setup fee for Enterprise?
No setup fee. Enterprise includes a dedicated onboarding session with a Customer Success Manager who will assist with CargoWise integration, custom n8n workflow setup, and SAT reporting configuration at no additional cost. Onboarding typically takes 2–3 business days.

Need a custom plan?

For volumes above 2,000 invoices/month or multi-entity setups, talk to our team about a tailored solution.
